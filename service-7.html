3PL Logistic Solutions

حلول اللوجستيات 3PL

We provide a comprehensive range of third-party logistics (3PL) solutions through our well-established logistics network, ensuring added-value services for our customers. Our 3PL offerings are designed to optimize your supply chain with services such as:

a). Warehousing – Secure storage for products

b). Transportation – Reliable delivery options

c). Freight forwarding – Seamless international shipping

d). Pick and pack – Accurate order fulfillment

e). Distribution – Timely delivery to end destinations

f). Track and trace – Real-time visibility of shipments

g). Cross docking – Streamlined transfer of goods

h). Contract packaging – Tailored packaging solutions

نحن نقدم مجموعة شاملة من حلول اللوجستيات من الطرف الثالث (3PL) من خلال شبكتنا اللوجستية الراسخة، مما يضمن خدمات ذات قيمة مضافة لعملائنا. تم تصميم عروضنا في 3PL لتحسين سلسلة التوريد الخاصة بك مع خدمات مثل:
